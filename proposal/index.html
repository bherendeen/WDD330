Site Plan - Home

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide an educational experience for children when learning more about the solar system in which they live. The site will be interactive to enhance their learning and help keep children engaged.

The site will be clean and easy to use. It will be responsive so users can access its content on multiple devices. Making the site easy to use will allow all users to use it with ease, no matter their age.
