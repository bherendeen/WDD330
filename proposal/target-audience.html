Site Plan - Audience

Target Audience

The space site's targeted audience is school children from 1st grade through 6th who want to learn a little more about the solar system they live in. This site will be easy to navigate and an additional tool for teachers to use when teaching their students.

  • Who: The site is for school children from 1st grade through 6th.
  • Age: 6 - 12
  • Use as: An additional teaching tool in school.
